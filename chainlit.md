## What is PWS?

**Problems Worth Solving** is a structured approach to innovation that ensures you're working on problems that matter. Instead of jumping to solutions, PWS helps you:

- Clarify what the **real problem** is (not symptoms)
- Validate that the problem **actually exists** with evidence
- Understand **who experiences** the problem and how
- Determine if **solving it matters** (impact, urgency, willingness to pay)
